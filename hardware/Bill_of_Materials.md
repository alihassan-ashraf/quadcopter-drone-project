## Hardware Summary

- **Frame:** F450 Quadcopter Frame (450mm)
- **Flight Controller:** Pixhawk 2.4.8
- **Motors:** 4 × A2212 1400KV Brushless Motors
- **ESCs:** 4 × 30A ESCs
- **GPS:** M8N GPS Module with Compass
- **Radio System:** FlySky FS-i6X + Receiver
- **Battery:** 3500mAh LiPo
- **Charger:** iMAX B6 Balance Charger
